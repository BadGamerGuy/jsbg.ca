Price & Ordering

$50-$60 + GST per board.

To order, email apologies@jsbg.ca.

Customization: custom board decorations and color choices are possible, but the price may increase. Reach out and we’ll work out the details.

Current limit: maximum 4 colors per side.

What you get

  • Travel-sized board with built-in storage.
  • 6 pegs for each player (extra 2 of each in case they get lost).
  • Standard deck of cards.

3D Printing Quality and Factory Seconds

  • There is some variability in 3d printing and while I'm constantly striving to improve the finish and consistency on my prints there are occasional slight imperfections in the prints. These will always be minimal and won't impact functionality in any way.
  • I do my best to be up front about this and don't hide the imperfections in the photos. I think the worst of them is the close up of the Classic. This is not a standard finish (you can compare to the other boards) but it might happen. If you have extremely high standards and require a more pristine then let me know and if you're willing to accept additional some costs for prints you deem unnacceptable then we can make it work.
  • All that being said, I too tend to have a little too high standards and will sometimes end up with "factory second" boards that I'm unwilling to give out. They still function fine but the quality isn't good enough for me to be willing to gift someone. If you're interested in the factory seconds let me know, they're $20 a set to allow me to recoup some costs however the sets might not match and I hopefully don't have much stock lol.
